**Materials:** PETG prints, brass inserts, CA gel, jigs  
**Process:**
1. Sand & prime surfaces
2. Join interlocks
3. Apply CA glue
4. Install inserts

**Notes:**  
- Perform in well-ventilated area  
- Wear nitrile gloves  
- Use laser alignment

**Philosophy:**  
- Monocoque design
